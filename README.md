LastPuff is a smart, all-in-one wellness platform that began as a smoking-cessation solution and evolved into a comprehensive health and fitness companion. It is designed to help users break unhealthy habits while actively guiding them toward a stronger, more balanced lifestyle through personalized goals, structured fitness plans, habit tracking, and detailed progress insights.

What truly sets LastPuff apart is the national recognition it received at the Smart India Hackathon (SIH)—India’s premier innovation challenge organized by the Government of India. 🏆 LastPuff is an SIH-winning project, selected for its innovation, scalability, real-world applicability, and strong social impact in the domain of public health and wellness. Competing against thousands of teams nationwide, the project stood out for its practical approach to preventive healthcare and sustainable habit change.

Built for both smokers and non-smokers, LastPuff adapts to individual user behavior using intelligent recommendations that promote consistency, accountability, and long-term well-being. Rather than offering generic solutions, it focuses on personalized, data-driven guidance that evolves with the user over time.

More than just an app, LastPuff represents an award-winning, government-recognized solution aimed at improving public health outcomes. Its SIH success validates not only the technical strength of the platform but also its potential to create meaningful, large-scale societal impact.
